# Dec 2 – Pre-Move Inspection Checklist (2561 Middlefield)

Use this checklist on **Dec 2** to make sure key work is finished before moving in.

## Closets – Completion Validation
- [ ] All bedroom closets have planned **shelving** installed (no missing shelves)
- [ ] Shelves are secure and level; brackets firmly attached
- [ ] Closet doors (if any) are installed and slide/swing smoothly
- [ ] No exposed nails/screws; edges are smooth and safe to touch
- [ ] Caulking/paint touch-ups around closet areas are complete
- [ ] Vacuum/clean inside closets after work is done
- [ ] Confirm with contractor: **Closet shelving scope is 100% complete** and no return visits are needed

## Whole-House Contractor Work – Interior Readiness
- [ ] **General cleaning**: interior swept/vacuumed, large debris removed, major dust from work cleared
- [ ] All work areas (closets, garage, around installations) are left in a clean, move-in-ready state
- [ ] Trash and construction materials are removed from the home (no loose boxes, tape, nails, etc.)
- [ ] **Backyard sliding doors** from **Family Room** and **Pool-Facing Bedroom** are cleaned and painted **white** (inside frames, handles wiped down, glass cleaned)

**Curtain Rods & Window Hardware**
- [ ] Curtain rods (or blinds hardware) installed on **all windows** per your plan
- [ ] Rods feel secure when gently tugged; brackets firmly anchored
- [ ] No obvious damage around rod brackets (chips, cracks, large drill holes)

**Ceiling Fans & Lighting**
- [ ] Ceiling fans installed and securely mounted in **all bedrooms and the office**
- [ ] Each fan turns on, spins smoothly, and has no wobble or unusual noise
- [ ] Fan light kits (if any) work in each room

**Dimmers & Switches**
- [ ] Dimmer switches installed in **all rooms** where requested
- [ ] Each dimmer turns lights on/off and dims smoothly without flicker
- [ ] Dimmer plate covers are installed and aligned correctly

**RO Filtration – Under-Sink Move (Old → New House)**
- [ ] Under-sink RO system has been disconnected at the **current house** per manufacturer/installer guidance
- [ ] All RO parts (tank, filters, faucet, fittings) were transported to **2561 Middlefield**
- [ ] RO faucet installed at the new kitchen sink (or chosen sink/location)
- [ ] Inlet, drain, and tank lines connected with no visible leaks
- [ ] RO system powers on (if applicable) and begins a flush/fill cycle as expected
- [ ] Old under-sink area at previous house left clean and capped/finished appropriately

## Garage – Epoxy Floor Completion
- [ ] Epoxy coating fully covers the garage floor (no bare concrete patches)
- [ ] Color/finish matches what was selected
- [ ] No sticky/tacky spots (floor feels dry to the touch)
- [ ] No major bubbles, peeling, or obvious defects
- [ ] Edges near walls and steps are coated cleanly
- [ ] Confirm with installer when it is safe to **walk** and **park a car** on the floor
- [ ] Take photos of the finished floor for your records

## Garage – Appliance & Utility Installation (Dec 2)

**Water Heater**
- [ ] Tank and connections show no signs of leaks
- [ ] Hot water is available at at least one nearby faucet
- [ ] Pressure relief valve and drain line are installed correctly
- [ ] Combustion venting (if gas) is secure and properly routed

**Water Softener / Water Filter**
- [ ] Units are installed in planned garage location
- [ ] Inlet/outlet pipes and bypass valve are clearly labeled and not leaking
- [ ] Power (if required) is available and units power on
- [ ] Manual / settings are available for later configuration

**Washer & Dryer (Garage)**
- [ ] Washer is level and does not rock
- [ ] Hot/cold water hoses are connected and show no leaks
- [ ] Drain hose is secured and properly placed
- [ ] Dryer vent is connected and secured (no kinks or major bends)
- [ ] Dryer powers on and drum spins when started

**Garage Refrigerator / Freezer**
- [ ] Fridge/freezer is level and in its planned garage location
- [ ] Door seals close properly
- [ ] Unit powers on (you can hear compressor or feel cooling after a few minutes)

**Garage Hanging Wall Mounts & Cabinets**
- [ ] All hanging wall mounts are securely anchored (no wobble when gently pulled)
- [ ] Garage cabinets are installed level and doors open/close smoothly
- [ ] No visible cracks or broken hardware

**Electrical – Sockets & EV Outlet**
- [ ] Standard garage outlets are installed with covers and show no damage
- [ ] Test at least one standard outlet with a small device (e.g., phone charger)
- [ ] **NEMA 14‑50 outlet** for EV charging is installed in the planned location
- [ ] Breaker for the NEMA 14‑50 is labeled correctly in the panel
- [ ] (If possible) Test the NEMA 14‑50 with an EV charger or tester, or confirm with electrician
