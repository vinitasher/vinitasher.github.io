Host Cue Sheet — Lady Whistledown

Event window: 11:00 AM – 2:00 PM • Ramp Walk ~12:00–12:30 PM

Before Guests Arrive

  • Place table cards (`1`,`2`,`3`) at host table for distribution.
  • Set up tally sheet and pen, keep a bell/soft flourish sound ready for reveals.
  • Arrange dressing table props and an assistant for quick help.

Practice Round (2–3 minutes)

Read the practice scandal from the Practice Handout. Show how tables raise cards and mark winners on the tally sheet.

Game Flow (per round)

  1. Announce the scandal and list the three suspects.
  2. Give tables 20–30 seconds to quietly discuss and choose.
  3. Count to three and prompt “Reveal!” — tables raise their card.
  4. Ring bell or play flourish, mark correct tables on tally sheet.
  5. Repeat for next scandal.

Wrap-up

  • When all rounds are complete, total table scores and announce the winner.
  • Use tie-breaker options if needed (sudden death scandal or Queen Charlotte’s Favor).

Quick Scripts

Intro: "Dearest readers, please make your way to your tables. We shall begin with a short practice scandal to warm up our senses."

Before Reveal: "Discuss quickly, choose your card, and on the count of three — Reveal!"

After Reveal: "Correct tables shall be noted in the Lady Whistledown ledger — mark your tally!"
