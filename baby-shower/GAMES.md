# Games Plan & Host Script

**Event:** Bridgerton-Themed Baby Shower for Vinit & Priyanka  
**Date:** November 22  
**Time:** 11:00 AM – 2:00 PM

---

## 1. Games Overview

Games are light, social, and themed around babies and Bridgerton-style fun. Use small prizes (tea tins, chocolates, candles) for winners.

Recommended schedule (flexible):
- **11:30 – 12:00 PM** – Ice-breaker games  
- **12:30 – 1:30 PM** – Main games & activity rotations (alongside painting stations)  

---

## 2. Game Details & Checklists

### 2.1 Don’t Say Baby

**Materials Checklist**
- [ ] Pins/ribbons/charms – one per guest  
- [ ] Basket or tray to hand them out  
- [ ] Small prize for winner  

**How to Play**
- At entry, give each guest one pin or charm.
- Announce that no one can say the word **"baby"** during the event.
- If someone hears another say "baby", they take that person’s pin.
- The guest with the most pins at 1:45 PM wins.

**Host Script**
> "Dearest guests, from this moment forth, the word 'baby' is strictly forbidden. Should you catch a fellow member of the Ton uttering it, you are most welcome to claim their pin. By the end of our gathering, the one with the greatest spoils shall be crowned the victor."  

---

### 2.2 Guess the Bottle

**Materials Checklist**
- [ ] 4–6 baby bottles  
- [ ] Different liquids (juice, water, flavored water, milk, etc.)  
- [ ] Number labels for each bottle  
- [ ] Answer sheets and pens  
- [ ] Prize for winner  

**How to Play**
- Fill each bottle with a different liquid and number them.
- Guests smell/look (no drinking unless you want a tasting version) and write down their guesses.
- Guest with the most correct answers wins.

**Host Script**
> "Here we have a most curious challenge for the senses. Before you lie a collection of baby bottles, each filled with a mysterious elixir. Rely on your keen Bridgerton instincts to guess their contents. The most accurate nose shall be duly rewarded."  

---

### 2.3 Baby Pictionary

**Materials Checklist**
- [ ] Bowl with baby-related prompts  
- [ ] Whiteboard/large paper and markers  
- [ ] Timer or phone stopwatch  
- [ ] Prize for winning team  

**How to Play**
- Divide guests into teams.
- One player picks a prompt and draws it without writing words or letters.
- Their team guesses within the time limit.
- Rotate players so everyone gets a turn.

**Host Script**
> "My dear artists of the Ton, it is time to let your quills and imaginations run wild. You shall draw scenes from the life of our soon-to-arrive little one, while your team attempts to decipher your creations. Fear not – even the most abstract scribbles will be met with applause."  

---

### 2.4 Price Is Right (Baby Edition)

**Materials Checklist**
- [ ] 5–8 baby items (diapers, wipes, lotion, onesie, pacifier, bottle, etc.)  
- [ ] Hidden list of real prices  
- [ ] Answer sheets and pens  
- [ ] Prize for winner  

**How to Play**
- Display items on a table.
- Guests guess the price of each item and write them down.
- Tally the difference from the real total.
- Closest to the actual total wins.

**Host Script**
> "Members of the Ton, even in high society, one must be mindful of the cost of raising an heir. Before you stand the essentials of babyhood. Place your best guesses as to their value, and we shall see who possesses the most practical knowledge of the market."  

---

### 2.5 Mingle Bingo

**Materials Checklist**
- [ ] Custom bingo cards with fun prompts  
- [ ] Pens  
- [ ] Prize for first winner (and optional second/third)  

**How to Play**
- Each guest receives a bingo card with prompts ("Has watched Bridgerton", "Is an older sibling", etc.).
- Guests mingle and find people matching prompts, collecting names/signatures.
- First to complete a line shouts the special phrase.

**Host Script**
> "To truly belong to the Ton, one must mingle. On your bingo cards you will find delightful prompts. Seek out your fellow guests, uncover their secrets, and when you complete a row, do declare with pride: 'My Dear Gentle Reader!'"  

---

### 2.6 Baby Hunt

**Materials Checklist**
- [ ] Small baby-themed items (mini pacifiers, tiny bottles, cutouts)  
- [ ] Basket or bag for each participant or team  
- [ ] Prize for winner  

**How to Play**
- Hide the items around the venue before guests arrive.
- At game time, give guests a short time limit to search.
- Guest or team that finds the most items wins.

**Host Script**
> "It appears the baby trinkets of the Ton have gone missing! You have but a short time to locate as many as you can. May your eyes be sharp and your steps swift."  

---

### 2.7 Mommy or Daddy?

**Materials Checklist**
- [ ] Printed question sheets  
- [ ] Pens  
- [ ] Prize for winner  

**How to Play**
- Guests answer who they think fits each statement: Mommy or Daddy.
- Vinit and Priyanka reveal their own answers.
- Guest whose answers match the couple’s most closely wins.

**Host Script**
> "Let us now turn our attention to the couple of the hour. Who will be the stricter parent? Who will sneak extra sweets to the little one? Place your bets, dear guests, as we discover whether your guesses match those of Vinit and Priyanka."  

---

### 2.8 Baby Predictions & Advice

**Materials Checklist**
- [ ] Prediction & advice cards  
- [ ] Pens  
- [ ] Decorated box or basket to collect cards  

**How to Play**
- Guests fill in predictions (arrival date, weight, traits) and advice.
- Cards are collected and later given to the parents-to-be.

**Host Script**
> "Every great story deserves a prophecy and a bit of wisdom. Kindly write down your predictions for Baby Shah and any words of advice for our beloved couple. These shall be treasured long after this season has ended."  

---

### 2.9 Paint the Bib

**Materials Checklist**
- [ ] Plain bibs  
- [ ] Fabric paints/markers  
- [ ] Brushes and water cups (if needed)  
- [ ] Table covers/newspapers  
- [ ] Aprons or napkins  

**How to Play**
- Guests design and decorate a bib for the baby.
- Encourage Bridgerton motifs, cute sayings, or simple colorful patterns.

**Host Script**
> "Artists of the Ton, it is time to leave your mark on the littlest member’s wardrobe. Create a bib worthy of high society—be it charming, witty, or simply adorable. One day, these will be worn with both drool and pride."  

---

### 2.10 Paint a Block

**Materials Checklist**
- [ ] Wooden blocks (sanded)  
- [ ] Non-toxic paints or markers  
- [ ] Table protection and cleaning supplies  

**How to Play**
- Guests paint letters, numbers, or designs on blocks.
- Together, they form a custom block set for the nursery.

**Host Script**
> "Let us construct the building blocks of Baby Shah’s future—quite literally. Paint a letter, a number, or a tiny illustration. In time, these blocks shall become part of the child’s earliest memories."  

---

### 2.11 Baby Word Search

**Materials Checklist**
- [ ] Printed word search sheets  
- [ ] Pens or pencils  
- [ ] Prize for winner  

**How to Play**
- Hand out word search sheets with baby- and Bridgerton-themed words.
- Give a time limit.
- First to finish or person with most completed words wins.

**Host Script**
> "For those whose minds delight in quiet puzzles, we present a word search of the finest order. Seek out the hidden words before the sand in our hourglass runs out, and claim your rightful victory."
