# Bridgerton-Themed Baby Shower Plan

**Honoring:** Vinit & Priyanka  
**Date:** November 22  
**Time:** 11:00 AM – 2:00 PM  
**Theme:** Regency-Era / Bridgerton-Inspired Soirée  
**Hosted by:** Lady Whistledown (Jinisha Shah) & Queen Victoria (Mayuri Shah)

---

## 1. Event Overview

A regal, pastel-soaked afternoon inspired by the world of Bridgerton, where guests are transported to a high-society gathering to celebrate the upcoming arrival of Baby Shah.

**Vibe:** Elegant, whimsical, romantic, and playful.  
**Dress Code:** "Regency Garden Party"
- Ladies: flowy dresses, pastel colors, lace, pearls, tiaras or floral headbands, gloves (optional)
- Gentlemen: shirts, vests/waistcoats, suspenders, bow ties (optional)
- Optional “prop bar” at entry with gloves, tiaras, fans, and faux pearls

---

## 2. High-Level Timeline (11:00 AM – 2:00 PM)

**11:00 – 11:30 AM** – Guest arrival, welcome, photos at backdrop, start "Don’t Say Baby"  
**11:30 – 12:00 PM** – High tea, refreshments, and a few games before lunch  
**12:00 – 12:30 PM** – Bridgerton ramp walk and photo session  
**12:30 – 1:00 PM** – Transition, final pre-lunch game or creative station  
**1:00 – 1:45 PM** – Lunch service (buffet)  
**1:45 – 2:00 PM** – Closing remarks, thank-you note, final photos

Times can be adjusted based on final guest count and venue constraints.

## 4. Special Activity: Bridgerton Ramp Walk (Overview)

A ramp walk among all the ladies wearing props that give them a Bridgerton look.

- Provide tiaras, faux pearls, lace gloves, fans, shawls, and small hats on a “Dressing Table”.  
- Play Bridgerton-style instrumental music during the walk.  
- Announce each participant as if they are being presented to the Ton.  
- End with a group photo at the backdrop with Vinit & Priyanka.

(Full decor details and suggested lines for the host are in [DECOR](./DECOR.html).)
