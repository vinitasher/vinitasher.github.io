# Food & Beverage Plan & Host Script

**Event:** Bridgerton-Themed Baby Shower for Vinit & Priyanka  
**Date:** November 22  
**Time:** 11:00 AM – 2:00 PM

---

## 1. Food & Timing Overview

The event will include a tea-style setup plus a full lunch.

**Suggested Food Timing:**
- **11:00 – 11:30 AM** – Welcome drinks / light nibbles (if any)  
- **12:15 – 1:00 PM** – Main lunch service  
- **1:00 – 1:30 PM** – Dessert/tea and relaxed mingling  

---

## 2. Lunch Menu (Main)

### 2.1 Chinese/Indo-Chinese Lunch

Planned lunch items:
- Chili Garlic Noodles  
- Manchurian  
- Hakka Noodles (Non-spicy)  
- Schezwan Fried Rice  
- Plain Fried Rice  

You can serve these in chafing dishes with name tags in a buffet line.

---

## 3. Tea & Beverage Setup

### 3.1 Core Items
- Tea cups  
- Tea sachets (Earl Grey, green tea, masala chai, chamomile, etc.)  
- Hot water dispensers or kettles  

### 3.2 Optional Add-ons
- Sugar, honey, lemon slices, milk  
- Flavored water or lemonade  
- A simple signature mocktail in a dispenser

---

## 5. Host Script for Food Moments

### 5.1 Pre-Lunch Announcement (around 12:15 PM)

> "Dearest gentle reader, the time has come to indulge in the finest delicacies of our little gathering. We invite you to make your way to the lunch buffet, where a delightful array of dishes awaits. Please help yourselves and return to your tables, for good food is best enjoyed with even better company."  

### 5.2 Introducing the Menu

> "Today’s luncheon offers a medley of flavors: Chili Garlic Noodles, rich and aromatic; comforting Manchurian; gentle Hakka Noodles for those who prefer a milder taste; and both Schezwan Fried Rice and Plain Fried Rice to complete the feast. May your plates be full and your hearts even fuller."  

### 5.3 Tea Time

> "For those inclined towards a more refined refreshment, our tea station stands ready. Choose from an assortment of fragrant tea sachets and allow yourself a quiet Bridgerton moment, teacup in hand, gossip most welcome."  

### 5.4 Closing Food Note (near 1:30–1:45 PM)

> "We hope the meal has pleased your palate as much as your presence has delighted our hearts. As we draw closer to the end of this gathering, may you carry the warmth of this table, and the joy shared for Vinit and Priyanka, into the seasons ahead."  

---

## 6. Quick Timeline Reference for Host

- **11:00 – 11:30 AM** – Offer welcome drinks, water, and light snacks (if arranged).  
- **12:15 – 1:00 PM** – Announce and serve lunch, keep buffet replenished.  
- **1:00 – 1:30 PM** – Tea/coffee, dessert, relaxed conversation.  
- **1:30 – 2:00 PM** – Final top-ups, ensure everyone has had enough to eat and drink.
# Decor Plan & Host Script

**Event:** Bridgerton-Themed Baby Shower for Vinit & Priyanka  
**Date:** November 22  
**Time:** 11:00 AM – 2:00 PM

---

## 1. Decor Overview

Theme: Soft, romantic Bridgerton-inspired Regency garden party with pastel colors and gold accents.

**Color Palette:** pastel pinks, lavender, baby blue, cream, white, with touches of gold.

---

## 2. Areas & Items

### 2.1 Entrance & Welcome
- **Entry banner with stand**
  - Suggested text: _"Dearest Gentle Reader, You Are Cordially Invited to Celebrate Vinit & Priyanka"_
  - Subtext: _"A Baby is Coming to the Ton – November 22"_
- Place near entry with a few balloons and artificial flowers.

### 2.2 Arch & Balloons
- **Arch** as a main focal point (behind parents-to-be seating or photo area).
- Balloons in pastel white, blush, lilac, light blue, with small gold accents.
- Optional faux greenery and flowers.

### 2.3 Photo Area
- **Photo backdrop with LED lights**
  - Sheer fabric backdrop (white/pastel) with warm LED curtain lights.
  - Central monogram or crest: _V & P_.
- **Photo booth**
  - Props: fans, tiaras, crowns, pearls, lace umbrellas, Regency quote placards.

### 2.4 Tables
- **Table covers** in pastel shades (lavender, baby blue, blush).
- **Table runners** in lace or satin (ivory/gold).
- **Table décor kettle pieces with artificial flowers**
  - Teapots or kettles as vases with faux roses, hydrangeas, baby’s breath.
  - Add small LED tealights around the centerpiece.

### 2.5 Extra Bridgerton Touches
- Printed “society papers” styled as Lady Whistledown gossip sheets.
- Soft classical/Bridgerton-style instrumental playlist.

---

**Entrance & Welcome**
- [ ] Entry banner printed and mounted on stand  
- [ ] Balloons/flowers at base of banner

**Tables**

**Atmosphere**
- [ ] Lady Whistledown “society papers” printed and placed  
- [ ] Music playlist queued and sound level checked  

---

## 4. Host Script (Decor Moments)

These lines can be used by any host/MC (Lady Whistledown or Queen Victoria) when highlighting the decor.

### 4.1 Welcome at Entrance (11:00–11:15 AM)

> "Dearest gentle reader, as you pass through this archway, you are officially entering the Ton’s most anticipated event of the season – the celebration of Vinit and Priyanka. Pray, do stop by our banner and allow the cameras to capture your entrance into this most delightful affair."

### 4.2 Highlighting the Photo Area

> "Should you wish to be immortalized in the society papers, do make your way to our splendid photo corner. With our royal crest, sparkling lights, and an abundance of Bridgerton-worthy props, your portrait shall be the talk of the season."

### 4.3 Table Decor Acknowledgement (during seating/lunch)

> "Kindly take a moment to admire the charming tea kettles and florals before you. Every detail has been arranged so that our dear Vinit and Priyanka may feel the full affection of the Ton on this special day."

### 4.4 Closing Decor Note (near the end)

> "As this gathering draws to a close, may these florals, twinkling lights, and memories serve as a gentle reminder that today, we did not merely decorate a room – we adorned a new chapter in the story of Vinit and Priyanka."
